# Credits & Attribution

Every framework, heuristic, and quoted line in this pack is the work of
**Paul Graham**, from his essays at **[paulgraham.com](https://www.paulgraham.com/articles.html)**.

These skills **distill and reframe** his ideas as operating instructions for AI
coding agents. They are a **derivative study aid** — not his words, and not a
substitute for the originals. **Independently created; not affiliated with,
endorsed by, or reviewed by Paul Graham.** No essay text is reproduced in full;
only brief anchor quotes are used. If the author requests changes or removal,
we'll comply.

**Read the originals — they're better than any distillation.**
